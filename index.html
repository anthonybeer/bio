About

“Set your goals high, and don’t stop till you get there.”
– Bo Jackson

Tony brings a calm competitive spirit to every team he has been a part of. Early on, Tony learned the power of goal setting and hard work. His first major life goal was to play division 1 soccer. He accomplished this goal and went on to be a 4 year starter at Butler University.

After graduating from Butler University with a degree in Finance. Tony started a career with Primerica Financial Services where he worked his way up to a regional manager. During this time, he honed he sales skills, recruited and trained new associates and learned how to be an effective speaker.

After 5 years in the finance industry, Tony began volunteering at his church in the kids ministry. This became the best part of his week and he fell in love working with kids. Gradually, Tony felt the pull to work with kids full time, and found he had a natural ability working with students with disabilities. His next major life goal happened when he was approached to build a district wide program for students with an Emotional Disturbances.

This started Tony on an amazing journey learning about emotions, and human behavior. His leadership skills became evident as he started to build teams who are able to handle any situation and help all students get regulated and develop the skills required to be successful.

If you are looking for a leader who is able to make decisions, develop systems, and get everyone on the team moving in the same direction, look no further. Tony is willing to help problem solve and come up with solutions.
